Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install templates?

All our templates come with detailed installation guides. For After Effects templates, simply open the .aep file in After Effects CC 2018 or later.

What are the system requirements?

Our templates require Adobe After Effects CC 2018+ or Adobe Premiere Pro CC 2019+. Most templates work best with 8GB+ RAM and a dedicated GPU.

Can I use templates for commercial projects?

Yes! All our templates come with a commercial license that allows you to use them in client projects, social media, and broadcast.

How often do you release new templates?

We release 10-15 new templates every month. Subscribers get early access to all new releases.
